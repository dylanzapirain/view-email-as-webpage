LinkedIn Posts for June 20th - 24th below

Monday 20th June: SLHD Promotional Video Post
For the 1st post on the SLHD LinkedIn, I have created a short showcase video with the theme being - "Looking for a new career in healthcare? Think Sydney Local Health District."

The primary target audience for the video is people who are entering the workforce for the first time. This targeting reflects a broad awareness building strategy for the District, with the goal of brining awareness of SLHD to LinkedIn users.

The video features footage from the construction of the Mass Vacination Centre, and of our staff having fun in a team environment, with overlayed text - "Build", "Develop" and "Thrive" at the District.
