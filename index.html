Overview for the upcoming week on LinkedIn
Good afternoon. For the upcoming week of the 27th of June, I have created the following content for the District's LinkedIn platform.
  • 1x short promo video for GradStart/MidStart Program
  • 1x 'inspirational quote style post'
  • 1x job promo video - Scrub Scout
  • 1x instructional video for LinkedIn job alerts
  • 1x 'aesthetic repost' from the Sydney Research page
If you would like to share any feedback please feel free to send me a message at any time or you are welcome to call me on 0427 962 272

All video content has come from previously recorded District messaging and audio has been used following licensing requirements of Canva. See here for further information.
LinkedIn Posts for June 27th - July 1st below

Monday 27th June: GradStart/MidStart Video Post
For the 1st post of the week on LinkedIn, I have created a short promotional video for the GradStart/MidStart program at SLHD. The video has fast-paced, upbeat music and prompts the viewer to start their nursing or midwifery career at SLHD.

The primary target audience for the video is nurses who are now considering where to apply for their GradStart program

The video features footage of a nurse confidently walking toward the camera. I felt this matched the messaging of the video and can help to draw the viewer's attention.

GradStart/MidStart Video
Tuesday 28th June: Suzanne Kennewell - Dietitians Australia's Award of Merit Inspirational Quote Post
For post #2 I created an 'inspirational quote' graphic to announce Suzanne Kennewell's Award of Merit.

The post takes a quote from Suzanne and aesthetically displays her voice. This post is designed to inspire followers and celebrate Suzanne's achievement.

Inspirational Quote Graphic
Wednesday 29th June: Video Job Promo - Scrub Scout
For the 3rd post of the week, I have created a video promotion for the Scrub Scout position.

The short video provides the viewer with the benefits of the opening and uses text animation to engage the attention of the viewer.

Scrub Scout Video
Thursday 30th June: Instructional Video - LinkedIn Job Alerts
For the 4th post of the week I wanted to inform the audience that they can turn on LinkedIn job alerts, this allows them to receive automatic alerts for when new jobs are posted to the SLHD LinkedIn

LinkedIn Job Alerts Video
Friday 1st July: Aesthetic repost of Research X-Factor Event
For the 5th post of the week, I have created a graphic to repost the Research X-factor event from the Sydney Research page.

This is to help bring awareness to the followers of the SLHD LinkedIn of the Sydney Research page and to inform viewers of the first Research X-factor event, in case they missed the original story.

Aesthetic Repost
